RAMEN

Ramen is a japanese adaptation of Chinese wheat noodles.

The noodles

In the old days, a single lump of dough was manually stretched and folded in half dozens of times til it formed a bunch of thin noodles.

There are 2 types of noodles; low or high alkaline and wavy or thick ramen noodles

Noodle Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Alkaline mineral water (kansui)

fun fact- It is the kansui which give Ramen noodles their unique bounce and taste and it also makes them yellow even though they contain no egg.

The Broth

Basic broth contains minimum ingredients, mainly just pork bones or chicken bones or both!

The Flavour

Broth + Flavouring = Soup

  • Shio - This means salt and is the traditional soup flavouring
  • Shoyo- This means soy sauce and is the next oldest flavour. The broth for this tends to not contain pork
  • Miso -
  • Tonkotsu - Contains either salt or soy sauce. Made by boiling ground up pork bones for 12-15 hours in the stock and leaves a whitish coloured broth
